FAQ

How do I invite the bot?

Click “Add to Discord” or use this link:
Invite Devil Bot.

What permissions are required?

For most features, invite with Administrator. To be granular: moderation needs Manage Roles/Messages/Kick/Ban; antinuke needs Manage Guild; prefix commands need Message Content.

How do I register commands faster?

Add the bot to your server and commands appear almost instantly if registered per-guild. Global registration can take up to an hour across Discord.

How do I clear global commands?

Re-register the bot’s commands for your server; they’ll replace old global ones after propagation.

Where are balances stored?

Balances and daily cooldowns persist on the bot’s host; they survive restarts but back up before moving hosts.

How do I configure antinuke?

Turn it on with /antinuke enable, whitelist trusted users/bots with /antinuke whitelist, and set thresholds with /antinuke limits.

Can I change the prefix?

Prefix is . by default; prefix and slash commands both work side by side.
